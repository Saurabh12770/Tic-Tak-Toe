Tik Tak Toe

Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic two-player, turn-based game played on a 3x3 grid. The objective is to get three of your marks (either an "X" or an "O") in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, before your opponent does. Players take turns marking one empty square at a time with their respective symbol. The game ends when one player wins. Tic-Tac-Toe is a simple yet fun game that tests strategy, quick thinking, and foresight.
